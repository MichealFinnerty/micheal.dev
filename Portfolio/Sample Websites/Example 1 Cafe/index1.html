Our Story

Three generations, one mill

The Mill House has stood beside the river since 1860. What was once a working flour mill is now home to our café — same stone walls, same water running past the window, just better coffee than the millers ever had.

Everything is made fresh each morning, from the bread to the scones, using local suppliers wherever we can.
