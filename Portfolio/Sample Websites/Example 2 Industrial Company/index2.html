Who We Are

01 / About

Sherwood Electrical has been doing the unglamorous parts of electrical work properly since 2014 — the earthing, the testing, the paperwork that actually matters when something goes wrong.

We're a small two-van operation, which means the person who quotes the job is the person who shows up to do it. No subcontractors, no call centre, no surprises on the invoice.

Every job is RECI-certified and signed off with a completion cert, whether it's a socket swap or a full rewire.
