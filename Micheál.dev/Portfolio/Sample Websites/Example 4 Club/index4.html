The Club

Est. 1924

Pallaskenry GAA has been at the heart of the parish for a hundred years — through county finals, lean years, and everything in between, the club has stayed the one constant.

We field teams from U6 right up to Senior across both hurling and football, run entirely by volunteers who give their evenings and weekends because the club matters to them.

Whether your child is picking up a hurl for the first time or you're a returning adult looking to get back playing, there's a place for you here.
